About

CFP Calculator is an app that was professionally produced to cater people who wants to know their Ideal Bodyweight with the Four formulas provided. Each formula has its own equations to compute a persons Ideal Bodyweight base on a person's height, weight and gender.

This was developed by the CFP group of companies which specializes on Health-related applications.

This calculator is free to use and is readily available for the public. To use the app simply head to the Calculator page and input the data needed.
